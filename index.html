Why Guests Choose MusArt

Walk Everywhere

Reach Piazza del Duomo, Santa Maria Novella Station, and the Uffizi Gallery in less than 10 minutes on foot, making MusArt ideal for weekend escapes and train travelers.

Comfort That Inspires

Each room pairs premium linens, smart climate control, and fast WiFi with curated art pieces sourced from Florentine studios so you feel at home while staying creative.

Concierge Service

We help you secure museum tickets, restaurant reservations, and day trips into Chianti so you can experience Florence like a local.

Things to Do Near MusArt

From our doorstep you can weave through artisan boutiques or hop on the tram to explore the whole city.

  • Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore: Climb Brunelleschi's dome for views across Florence. Official site
  • Mercato Centrale: Sample Tuscan pastries, cheeses, and espresso just a short walk away.
  • Ponte Vecchio: Sunset strolls across the Arno's most iconic bridge, lined with jewelers and musicians.
  • Tram T2 stop: Direct connection to Florence Airport for stress-free departures.

About MusArt

Welcome to MusArt, a charming Bed & Breakfast located in the heart of Florence, just minutes from the historic center and Santa Maria Novella Station.

Our B&B offers elegantly decorated rooms that combine comfort with artistic touches, creating a unique and memorable stay for our guests. Each room is thoughtfully designed to provide a relaxing atmosphere after a day of exploring the beautiful city of Florence.

At MusArt, we believe that hospitality is an art form. Our team is dedicated to ensuring your stay is perfect, offering personalized service and local insights to help you make the most of your Florentine experience.
